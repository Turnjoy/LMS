About Turnjoy LMS

Turnjoy LMS is designed to separate the marketing front-door from every school portal. Each school operates under its own domain with dedicated admin, teacher, and student dashboards.

Secure Tenant Routing

School domains are resolved at request time and routed directly to the tenant's portal experiences.

Public Marketing

The landing pages on the root domain remain purely marketing-focused, with no administration leak.

Flexible Subject Setup

Each school selects its own subject offerings and streams independently, including CRS, IRS, or both.

School Section Roles

Primary and Secondary admin permissions are separated, with special dashboards for each section when both are configured.
